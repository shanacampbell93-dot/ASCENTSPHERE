How I Started Coding & Why I Built Ascent Sphere

Founder Story | Shana


I didn’t start coding as an adult with a perfect plan. I had already been building, experimenting, and creating digital projects long before I ever enrolled in a formal diploma.

In many ways, I’ve been doing this since I was younger — exploring ideas, systems, and digital creation long before I had language for what it actually was.

But what brought me back to coding properly was a realisation: I had been trying to build a life that fit around survival, responsibility, and being a single parent — instead of focusing on what I was actually naturally drawn to and capable of building.

When I looked closely at my interests and patterns, everything pointed back to the same place: digital systems, writing, creativity, and communication.


Why Ascent Sphere Exists

Ascent Sphere is a digital ecosystem designed to connect learning, services, tools, and opportunity into one structured system.

I wanted to reduce the fragmentation of knowledge — where everything is scattered, expensive, or inaccessible — and create something more unified and usable.


The Long-Term Vision

This is not just a website. It is an evolving system of tools, resources, and services designed to support people building new paths forward.

My goal is to build something sustainable that creates opportunity, clarity, and access for people who are starting from different circumstances.


For me, this is just the beginning.
